WE ARE ROSEN SHINGLE CREEK.

Rosen Shingle Creek is nestled on a 255-acre site along Shingle Creek just off Universal Boulevard, east of the Orange County Convention Center North/South expansion and just 10 minutes away from the Orlando International Airport. Our ideal location is a short distance to a variety of Orlando’s best attractions, restaurants, shopping and entertainment venues.

Our 1,501 guest rooms offer luxurious first-class settings that capture the hotel environment. Capitalizing on Rosen Shingle Creek’s elevation, every single room offers a stunning view of the golf course and the area’s incomparable setting.
